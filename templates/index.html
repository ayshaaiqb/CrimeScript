Welcome to Crime Scripting


Crime scripting is a simple and effective crime modeling technique that aims to improve understanding of security analysts about security and crime incidents.


They are written in the form of definiting a problem, listing the pre-conditons, during the crime conditons and post-conditons. Then the goal of the criminals is analysed in order to mitigate the requirements the criminal needs to fulfil.


This web application looks at three types of crime scripting methods: Narrative and Tables.


Narrative scripts tend to be simple text files that list the step, Tables will be formatted in a table form.
